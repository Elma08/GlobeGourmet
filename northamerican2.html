American Pancakes

Quantity
Ingredient
1 1/2 cups
Plain flour
1/4 cup
Caster sugar
2 tsp
Baking powder
1/4 cup
Cooking oil or melted butter
2 tsp
Vanilla extract
2
Large eggs
1 cup
Milk
Maple syrup to serve

  1. In a large mixing bowl, sift the flour, sugar and baking powder. Give it a little whisk.
  2. Next, add your cooking oil or melted butter into a bowl. Add your vanilla, eggs and milk to your mixture and stir to combine
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture and gently fold through. The mixture should be nice and thick.
  4. Heat a small frying pan on a medium-low heat, add a litter butter to the pan and pour over 3 heaped tablespoons of your pancake batter. Heat for 1-2 minutes or until golden brown, and then flip.
  5. Heat on the opposite side for about 1 minute or until also golden. Remove from the heat and set aside. Repeat with the remaining pancake batter.
  6. Serve pancakes immediately with maple syrup.
