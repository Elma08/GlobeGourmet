Spanish Paella

Quantity
Ingredient

1
Onion, sliced
2
Garlic cloves, crushed
110g
Chorizo
1 tbsp
Smoked paprika
300g
1 litre
Hot chicken stock
200g
Frozen peas
500g
Clams
6
Jumbo raw prawns, in shells
3 tbsp
Chopped fresh flat leaf parsley
1
Lemon, cut into wedges
Sea salt and freshly ground pepper

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a heavy-based frying pan and cook the onion and half of the garlic for five minutes, stirring regularly.
  2. Add the chorizo and fry until it releases its oil, then stir in the smoked paprika and rice.
  3. Keep stirring until the rice is coated in oil before pouring in the stock. Bring to the boil and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add the clams and peas. Cook for another five minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the clam shells open. Discard any clams that don't open.
  5. In a separate plan, heat the remaining oil and garlic. Add the prawns and fry quickly for a minute or two, and then add them into the paella.
  6. Add salt and pepper, to taste. Stir through the parsley, flakes of salmon and garnish with the lemon wedges. Serve immediately.
