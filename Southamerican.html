South America is a continent located in the Western Hemisphere, primarily in the Southern Hemisphere, and is known for its breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and diverse ecosystems.

This continent is rich in culinary traditions and flavours.

Its diverse geography and cultural heritage have given rise to a wide array of delicious recipes

South America consists of twelve independent countries: Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Ecuador, Guyana, Paraguay, Peru, Suriname, Uruguay, and Venezuela.

The official languages vary across the continent, with Spanish being the predominant language in most countries, except for Brazil, where Portuguese is spoken.
